Prototype hub — every concept from the ideation doc, playable. All offline, one hand, English. Progress saves locally.
FLAGSHIP — final concept
◉ Echo Orbit

Tap to leap orbit to orbit, forever upward. Your best runs fly beside you as Echoes and earn for you. Full meta: upgrades, prestige (Supernova), daily challenges, simulated rewarded ads.

 V0 — rejected concept
▦ Sky Stack

The first idea: tap to stack swinging blocks. Fun for a minute — play it to feel why the investor critique rejected it as derivative and shallow.

 Variant B
✿ Loop Garden

Draw loops around drifting fireflies to capture their light and grow an everlasting garden. Zen and lovely — but a two-dimensional gesture, weaker one-thumb fit.

 Variant C
≈ Tidal

Hold to pull the tide, release to send a wave that pushes shells past the treasure line. Satisfying, but the skill ceiling is low — you watch more than you play.
